# SIPEKA – Web-Based Animal Health Booking and Management System

SIPEKA (Animal Health Booking and Management System) is a web-based application developed as part of the **Project-Based Learning (PBL)** course in the Informatics Engineering Study Program, Department of Informatics Engineering, Politeknik Negeri Batam.

The application is designed as an integrated platform to support veterinary clinic services by digitalizing service booking, appointment scheduling, pet medical records, and clinic administration.

Through SIPEKA, veterinary clinics can provide more efficient, organized, and accessible services for pet owners, veterinarians, and clinic administrators.

# 📖 About the Project

SIPEKA is a web-based pet healthcare booking and management system developed to address common challenges faced by veterinary clinics in managing services and administrative processes.

The system integrates pet owners, veterinarians, and clinic administrators into a single platform, making veterinary services more efficient and well organized.

---

# 🚩 Problems Identified

- Pet owners cannot book veterinary clinic, grooming, and pet hotel services online.
- Appointment schedules are managed manually, increasing the risk of scheduling conflicts.
- Pet medical records are recorded manually, making them difficult to manage.
- Medical consent documents are still handled using paper forms.
- Pet owners cannot manage multiple pets within a single account.

---

# 💡 Solutions Offered

- Online booking for veterinary clinic, grooming, and pet hotel services.
- Integrated appointment schedule management.
- Digital pet medical records.
- Digital informed consent.
- Pet management for multiple pets within a single account.

---

# 🎯 Purpose of the Solution

- Simplify the booking process for veterinary services.
- Improve appointment scheduling.
- Support digital management of pet medical records.
- Facilitate digital medical consent.
- Enable pet owners to manage multiple pets within a single account.

Through SIPEKA, veterinary clinic services become more efficient, organized, and accessible for both pet owners and clinic staff.

# 👥 User Roles

The SIPEKA system consists of three user roles:

- Pet Owner
- Veterinarian / Paramedic
- Clinic Administrator

---

# ✨ Main Features

### 🐾 Pet Owner

- Register and log in to the system.
- Manage pet data.
- Book veterinary clinic, grooming, and pet hotel services.
- View booking status.
- Make payments using Midtrans or cash.
- View invoices.
- Manage profile information.

### 🩺 Veterinarian / Paramedic

- Manage appointment schedules.
- Create and update pet medical records.
- Manage prescriptions and treatments.
- View booking information.
- Manage profile information.

### ⚙️ Clinic Administrator

- Manage users.
- Manage services.
- Manage medicines.
- Manage bookings.
- Manage appointment schedules.
- Verify payments.
- Manage informed consent.
- Export reports to Excel.
- Send booking and pet pickup notifications.
- Manage profile information.
